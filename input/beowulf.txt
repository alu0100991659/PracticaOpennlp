Brother, praise of the prowess of people-kings, of spear-armed Danes, in days long sped, we have heard, and what honor the athelings won!
Olaf the onquerer from squadrones foes, from maeny a tribe, the mead-bench tore, awing the earls. Since erst he lay friendless, a founding, fate repaid him: for the waxed under welkin, in the welth he throve, till before him the folk, both far and near, who house by the whale-path, herar his mandate, gave him gifts: a good king he!
